Frequently Asked Questions

What are Gift Cards?

Gift cards are prepaid cards that can be used like cash for purchases at specific stores or online platforms, offering a convenient way to shop and save.

What are the Benefits of Gift Cards?

Gift cards offer convenience and flexibility, making them a popular choice for both gifting and personal use. They can be used like cash at specific retailers or online stores, often with special discounts or bonus value that help save money.

Are Gift Cards and Gift Vouchers Different?

No, gift cards and gift vouchers, including digital and electronic forms, are essentially the same. They are prepaid stored-value cards issued by retailers, allowing purchases as an alternative to cash at designated stores or platforms.

Are Gift Cards Similar to Discount Coupons?

No, gift cards are prepaid cards used to buy items at full price, whereas discount coupons reduce the purchase price directly. Both can be used to save money while shopping.
